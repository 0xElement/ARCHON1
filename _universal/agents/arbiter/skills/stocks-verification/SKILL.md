# Stock Analysis Verification

Independent verification of stock analysis quality and accuracy.

## What Gets Verified
- **CMP accuracy** — does the price match real market data (within 2%)?
- **P/E ratio math** — is it consistent with EPS and CMP?
- **Technical indicators** — are RSI, MACD, MA values present and real?
- **Support/resistance levels** — specific ₹ values identified?
- **Bull + bear cases** — both sides presented, not one-sided?
- **Stop loss** — defined with specific ₹ value?
- **Risk:Reward ratio** — mathematically sound?
- **Recommendation** — BUY/SELL/HOLD justified by the analysis?
- **Contrarian view** — at least one challenger perspective included?
- **Data sources** — real and verifiable, not hallucinated?
